Barley Growing Process

Seeding

1. Prepare the soil by tilling and removing weeds.

2. Sow the barley seeds evenly across the prepared soil.

3. Water the seeds adequately and ensure the soil remains moist.

Growth

4. Barley will begin to germinate within a week.

5. As the plants grow, provide regular watering and ensure adequate sunlight.

6. Watch for any signs of pests or diseases and take necessary measures.

Harvesting

7. Wait for the barley plants to reach maturity, usually around 90-120 days.

8. Check the moisture content of the grains. It should be around 12-14% for harvesting.

9. Cut the plants at the base and tie them into bundles.

10. Hang the bundles upside down in a well-ventilated area to dry.
