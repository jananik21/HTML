Crop Information: Wheat

Stage Duration Tasks
Seeding 2 weeks
  • Prepare the soil for planting
  • Sow the wheat seeds
  • Ensure adequate moisture and sunlight
Growth 8 weeks
  • Monitor and maintain proper irrigation
  • Apply fertilizers for optimal growth
  • Manage weed control
Ripening 2 weeks
  • Monitor the crop for signs of ripening
  • Protect from pests and diseases
  • Harvest when grains are fully mature
Harvesting 1 week
  • Cut the wheat stalks near the ground
  • Gather the harvested wheat
  • Store and process as needed
