Fruits growing proces

Seeding

Start by planting the seeds in nutrient-rich soil. Ensure that the seeds are placed at the appropriate depth and water them regularly.

Sprouting

After a few weeks, the seeds will start to sprout, and you'll see small seedlings emerging from the soil.

Growth

The seedlings will continue to grow, developing leaves, stems, and roots. Make sure to provide adequate sunlight and water for healthy growth.

Step 4: Flowering

As the plants mature, they will produce flowers, which are necessary for pollination and fruit development.

Step 5: Fruit Formation

After successful pollination, the flowers will transform into fruits. Watch as the fruits grow and change in color and size.

Step 6: Harvesting

Once the fruits have ripened, carefully harvest them from the plants. Enjoy the fruits of your labor!
