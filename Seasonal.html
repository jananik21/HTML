Seasonal Guidelines for Crops

Spring Crops (March to May)

  • Tomatoes - Plant in early spring after the last frost.
  • Lettuce - Sow seeds in early spring for a cool-season crop.
  • Peas - Plant in early spring for a spring harvest.

Summer Crops (July to August)

  • Corn - Plant in late spring or early summer when the soil has warmed up.
  • Watermelon - Plant in late spring for a summer harvest.
  • Beans - Sow seeds in late spring or early summer.

Fall Crops (September to November)

  • Pumpkins - Plant in late summer for a fall harvest.
  • Carrots - Sow seeds in late summer or early fall for a fall crop.
  • Broccoli - Plant in late summer or early fall for a fall or winter harvest.

Winter Crops (December to February)

  • Spinach - Sow seeds in late summer or early fall for a winter harvest.
  • Kale - Plant in late summer or early fall for a winter crop.
  • Cabbage - Plant in late summer or early fall for a winter harvest.
