Forage Crop Growth

Seeding

Seeding is the initial stage of forage crop growth. It involves planting seeds in prepared soil.

Germination

After seeding, the seeds absorb water and nutrients from the soil, leading to germination. Roots and shoots begin to develop.

Vegetative Growth

During this stage, the forage crop focuses on leaf and stem growth. It requires ample sunlight, water, and nutrients.

Flowering

When the plant reaches maturity, it produces flowers. These flowers are important for pollination and seed production.

Maturation

As the forage crop continues to grow, the seeds mature and develop
