Vegetable Growing Process

Seeding

1. Prepare the soil by removing any weeds and adding compost.

2. Plant the seeds according to the recommended depth and spacing.

3. Water the soil gently to keep it moist.

Growth and Care

1. Provide regular watering to keep the soil consistently moist.

2. Apply fertilizer as per the vegetable's specific requirements.

3. Remove any weeds that compete with the vegetable plants.

4. Protect the plants from pests and diseases using organic or chemical methods.

Harvesting

1. Monitor the vegetables for signs of maturity, such as color and size.

2. Use gardening tools to harvest the vegetables carefully.

3. Store or use the harvested vegetables as desired.
